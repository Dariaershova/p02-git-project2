The Echeveria amoena or Red-Beaked Succulent, is a pale green succulent of the Crassulaceae family. Native to Mexico and Central America, these succulents need a fair amount of sunshine, but require little water. Water these plants from the roots to avoid over-watering. If the tips begin to yellow, water the plant more frequently. The leaves may also spread wide if there is not enough sunlight.
