The Echeveria sirius or "Lotus" Succulent, is a red-tipped, spiky varient of the Crassulaceae family which resembles a lotus flower. These succulents are native to southern California, and require quite a bit of sunshine, but don't need a lot of water. Water these plants from the roots to avoid over-watering. If the tips begin to yellow, water the plant more frequently. The leaves may also spread wide if there is not enough sunlight.
